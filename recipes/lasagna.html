Homemade lasagna recipe

If you're looking for a homemade lasagna recipe that's rich and comforting, you're in the right place. This classic dish combines layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy cheese for the ultimate family meal.

Learn how to make the best lasagna with simple ingredients, find out how to layer it properly, and get tips for baking it to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herbs (basil, oregano)

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
    • Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
    • Add ground beef or sausage, cook until browned.
    • Stir in tomato sauce and herbs, simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Cook the noodles
    • Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
    • Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta, parmesan, salt, and pepper.
    • Mix until smooth.
  4. Layer the lasagna
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Place a layer of noodles, followed by ricotta mixture, sauce, and mozzarella.
    • Repeat layers until dish is full, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake the lasagna
    • Cover with foil and bake at 190°C (375°F) for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake an additional 20 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  6. Rest and serve
    • Let the lasagna rest for at least 10 minutes before slicing.
    • Serve hot and enjoy!
