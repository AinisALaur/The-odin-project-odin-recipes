Homemade pizza recipe

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners, you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim - and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame.

Learn how to make the best pizza crust of your life with just a few ingredients, find out how to shape the dough, and get our best storage secrets.

Ingridients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
    • Mix flour, yeast, salt, sugar, and warm water.
    • Knead until smooth and elastic.
    • Let it rise in a warm place until it doubles in size.
  2. Preheat the oven
    • Heat oven to about 220°C (430°F), or as hot as it goes if using a pizza stone.
  3. Shape the dough
    • Punch down the risen dough.
    • Roll or stretch it into a thin, round base on a floured surface.
  4. Prepare the baking tray or stone
    • Lightly oil or dust with cornmeal to prevent sticking.
    • Transfer the shaped dough onto it.
  5. Add the sauce
    • Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base.
    • Leave a small border around the edges for the crust.
  6. Add toppings
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese.
    • Add toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, peppers, olives, or fresh basil.
  7. Bake the pizza
    • Place in the preheated oven.
    • Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
