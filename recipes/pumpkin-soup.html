Homemade Pumpkin Soup Recipe

This creamy pumpkin soup is simple, comforting, and perfect for chilly days. It's full of flavor and easy enough for beginners to prepare.

Learn how to make smooth pumpkin soup with basic ingredients, discover how to season it perfectly, and get tips for serving it warm and cozy.

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin (peeled and chopped)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrot
  • Vegetable or chicken broth
  • Olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Nutmeg (optional)
  • Cream (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Prepare the vegetables
    • Peel and chop the pumpkin, carrot, and onion.
    • Minced the garlic cloves.
  2. Sauté aromatics
    • Heat olive oil or butter in a pot over medium heat.
    • Add onion and garlic, sauté until soft and fragrant.
  3. Cook the vegetables
    • Add pumpkin and carrot to the pot.
    • Pour in broth until vegetables are covered.
    • Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until tender (about 20 minutes).
  4. Blend the soup
    • Use a hand blender or transfer to a blender to puree until smooth.
    • Return to the pot and reheat if necessary.
  5. Season and serve
    • Add salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg to taste.
    • Serve hot with a swirl of cream if desired.
